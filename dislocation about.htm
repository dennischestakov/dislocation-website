Dislocation

Dislocation is a 3D Puzzle game where you, the player, must traverse the world
by solving puzzles with the press of a button. You have to ability to "dislocate"
objects from their original position by moving them in a certain direction,
changing their direction, or even moving them through time.

However, your powers aren't invincible as certain obstacles can not be dislocated,
and a lot of enemies will try to stop you. The price has not been decided, and nor
has the release date. It is still very early in development, but if you want to sign
up for testing, go to the Testing tab. Check above for more info about me.
