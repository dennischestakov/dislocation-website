Home

Welcome to the Dislocation Homepage! This is an indie puzzle game being worked on by Dennis Chestakov.
It is still being made, but you can sign up to test at the Testing tab! You can learn more about me or
the game at the About tab, or you can visit the Discord server made for the game at the Discord tab!

Story

You wake up on an early morning to a loud bang. You quickly get up to see everyone is still asleep.
Looking outside, you see that your home village's windmill has burst into flames. You rush over and find
a weird glove on the ground. You pick it up, but before you can turn around to get out, you feel a hard
blow to your head, and you pass out.

After waking up, you will soon learn everything around you has changed dramatically. You must stop the
evil force that as awaken, and you must go through worlds, solving puzzles to get through them. Along the
way you will make allys and enemies on your journey through the world of Dislocation.
